About PREARN Pralancing Arena

This arena is designed as a sandbox for students and young professionals to experience real freelancing-like work in a guided, gamified environment. Each department field has its own problem statement, resources, and evaluation rubrics, making it easier to explore what you actually enjoy doing.

FAQs

Can I participate in more than one department?

Yes, you can register for multiple fields, but make sure you can commit to the timelines and submission requirements for each.

How will I receive prize money?

Prize money will be transferred via UPI/bank transfer after verification. Additional goodies, certificates, or features may be distributed digitally or physically depending on the event.
