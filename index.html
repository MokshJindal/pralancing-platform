About PREARN Pralancing Arena

This arena is designed as a cultural yet modern sandbox for students and young professionals to taste real freelancing-like work. Each department field carries its own dharma, problem statement, and evaluation, making it easier to explore what you truly enjoy while staying rooted in aesthetics and values.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I participate in more than one department field?

Yes. You may register for multiple arenas (Manthan, Shilp, Tattva, Yantra) as long as you can honour the timelines and submission requirements for each.

How will prize money (prasāda) be given?

Prasāda will be shared via UPI or bank transfer after verification of winners. Additional goodies and certificates may be sent digitally or physically depending on the event logistics.
