What is PREARN?

PREARN is not just another hackathon or internship portal. It is a proving ground where your skills are tested against real-world challenges and judged by companies, founders, and experts.

Every contest is a story. Every submission is a chapter. You don’t just earn money—you earn proof of work, recognition, and future opportunities.

For Students & Learners

Turn your assignments and side projects into a portfolio that pays. Work on curated problems from real companies and get paid if you're among the top performers.

For Startups & Companies

Post your real problems as structured battles. Get tens or hundreds of solution approaches, UI mocks, automations, campaigns, or code from motivated learners.
